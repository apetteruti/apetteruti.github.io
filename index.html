Amanda Petteruti


Full-Stack Web Developer


Amanda Petteruti is enrolled in the George Washington University Coding Bootcamp to learn to become a full stack web developer. She has a background in data and policy analysis, performance management, communications, and research. She has experience in juvenile, criminal justice and education policy and has written extensively on these subjects. She has worked at non-profits and government agencies, inlcuding the DC Department of Youth Rehabilitation Services and the Justice Policy Institute. Petteruti has a Master of Arts in education policy and leadership from the University of Maryland College Park and a Bachelor of Arts in sociology from Bates College. She is a softball player, avid reader, and occassional gardener.
